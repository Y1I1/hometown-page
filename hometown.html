Get ready to be charmed by Coventry, and its leafy surrounding Warwickshire countryside. Explore our city and uncover a place of bold innovation, lively culture and powerful history. From stunning architecture and creativity to fascinating stories of days gone by, there are opportunities for adventure at every turn, with must-visit attractions and sensational experiences aplenty.

Ice skate

Planet Ice Coventry is a cool ice skating rink that's great for families, offering open skate and family sessions, special children's sessions, birthday parties and Disco evenings! Plenty of options to choose from so you can pick the character of the session you attend. Helpful if you've got overexcitable kids!

Visit Coventry Cathedral

Visitors are always welcome at Coventry Cathedral and they have a trained team of Welcomers, Guides and Chaplains to help you feel comfortable in your exploration. You don't need to be of any particular faith to enjoy a visit here nor in fact of any faith at all: it's as beautiful an experience from an architectural perspective as it is from a religious one.

Herbert Art Gallery

Discover what life was really like in Coventry through the centuries, through personal stories, historical films and our amazing collections. Wander through dazzling interactive displays and investigate the natural world, modern art, and the old masters in our eight permanent galleries. Explore a wide range of exhibitions, many from prestigious national museums, in the temporary exhibition spaces.
